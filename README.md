## Projects

### Customer Churn Analysis
Analyzed customer churn data using SQL in BigQuery to identify key drivers of retention and customer attrition.

**Tools:** SQL (BigQuery)

**Key Highlights:**
- Calculated overall churn rate (~26.5%), identifying a significant retention challenge  
- Found month-to-month customers had the highest churn (~42%), indicating low contract commitment risk  
- Identified ~3x higher churn among customers without tech support (41% vs 15%)  
- Determined new customers (0–12 months) are most at risk (~47% churn), highlighting onboarding gaps

### Job Market Skills Analysis
Analyzed 2,000+ data analyst job postings to identify in-demand skills, salary trends, and hiring patterns.

**Tools:** Python, pandas, matplotlib, seaborn

**Key Highlights:**
- Identified SQL, Excel, and Python as the most in-demand skills across job postings  
- Analyzed common skill combinations to understand employer expectations  
- Examined geographic distribution of roles across major cities  
- Evaluated salary trends, showing most roles fall within the $50K–$80K range

### Retail Sales Analysis
Analyzed retail transaction data to uncover purchasing patterns and key revenue drivers.

**Tools:** Python, pandas, matplotlib, seaborn

**Key Highlights:**
- Identified clothing as the highest purchase frequency category  
- Found minimal correlation between customer age and purchase amount  
- Analyzed relationship between purchase quantity and total transaction value
