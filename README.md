## Projects

### Customer Churn Analysis
Analyzed customer churn data using BigQuery SQL to identify retention patterns and key drivers of customer attrition.

**Tools:** SQL (BigQuery)

**Key Highlights:**
- Calculated overall churn rate (~26.5%)
- Identified high churn among month-to-month customers (~42%)
- Found customers without tech support churn significantly more (~41% vs ~15%)
- Analyzed customer tenure, showing highest churn in first 12 months (~47%)

### Job Market Skills Analysis
Analyzed 2,000+ job postings to identify in-demand skills, salary trends, and hiring patterns for data analyst roles.

**Tools:** Python, pandas, matplotlib, seaborn

**Key Highlights:**
- Identified SQL, Excel, and Python as the most in-demand skills
- Analyzed common skill combinations across job postings
- Examined geographic job distribution across major cities
- Explored salary trends showing most roles fall within the $50K–$80K range

### Retail Sales Analysis
Analyzed retail transaction data to uncover purchasing patterns and customer behavior.

**Tools:** Python, pandas, matplotlib, seaborn

**Key Highlights:**
- Identified highest purchase frequency in clothing category
- Found weak correlation between age and purchase amount
- Analyzed relationship between quantity purchased and total spend
